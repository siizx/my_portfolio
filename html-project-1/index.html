Simple Crepes

Other recipes

Quick, easy and tasty crepes, to eat with whatever you want! But I suggest you NUTELLA!

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes.
  • Cooking time: 30 minutes.
  • Cost: Cheap

Ingredients:

  • Eggs: 3
  • Flour: 250 gr
  • Milk: 500 ml
  • Butter: 40 gr

How to make it:

  1. Mix the eggs and milk in a bowl, then add the flour and stir.
  2. Heat up a pan and add a very small amount of butter. Let it melt.
  3. When the butter is starting to boil on the pan, add enough mixture in the pan to make a thin layer with it.
  4. Cook at low to medium heat and flip the crepe when one side is brownish.
